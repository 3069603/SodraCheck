Checking Your Pension Record in Lithuania

If you worked officially in Lithuania — you may already have a pension record. Even after leaving the country, these years can give you entitlement to benefits.

Real Example

Situation

A person worked in Lithuania for 6 years under an employment contract and then left the country.

What the check revealed
  • 6 years of insurance record
  • all contributions saved in the system
  • partial pension possible at retirement age

The key is to keep the documents from the system in advance.

Check in 12 Seconds

Answer a few questions.

  • Did you work officially in Lithuania?
  • Did your employer pay taxes and social contributions?
  • Did you have an employment contract?
  • Did you work for more than 1 year?

If you answered "Yes" at least once — you probably already have a pension record in the system.
